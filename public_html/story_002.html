ARTIST FEATURE

Bound Species

Works by

Jennifer Latour

Jennifer Latour was born in Seven Islands, Quebec and now resides in Vancouver, Canada. She moved to the UK in 2003 where she became a self taught Makeup Fx artist having worked on various film productions across North America and Europe. 


During the first lock down of 2020 she put her knowledge of character creation and shape manipulation to practice through still life photography where she developed her unique style of floral arrangement. She signed with Open Doors Gallery (UK) in 2021 and is currently exhibited in London's Wembley Park outdoor art show “Figures of Change”.


Her first book featuring Bound Species is available for pre-sale
