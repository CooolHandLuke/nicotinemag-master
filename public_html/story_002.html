SHORT FILM

All I Got

By

Stillblue


“All I Got,” a short film by artist Stillblue, explores the raw emotions of beauty, pain and unyielding fervor, echoing the haunting refrain, "All I got is love, all they give is pain."  It unveils the profound beauty that resides within, the relentless pain that sometimes accompanies it, and the unwavering ardor that fuels our personal odysseys. At its core, this film is a poignant exploration of the human spirit.


Direction, Shot & Edit: Floris Henricus Koopman

Co-Direction & Performance: Stillblue

Fashion & Art Direction: Caspar Heineman
